Sustainable Fashion

What is sustainable fashion?

Sustainable fashion is fashion, or clothes, shoes, and accessories that are environmentally friendly. This means:

  • the product is produced using natural resources, such as water, energy, land, and soil, as sparingly and efficiently as possible to reduce the amount used
  • while the product is being produced, the energy used to produce it is renewable
  • the product is as conducive to being reused, repaired, or recycled as possible

The other aspect of sustainable fashion is making sure the conditions for all those involved are ethical and optimal. This includes workers in positions such as:

  • field work
  • factory work
  • transportation
  • sales
